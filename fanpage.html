Lea Salonga Superfan Page!

Multiple award-winning actress and singer Lea Salonga is renowned across the world for her powerful voice and perfect pitch. She is best known as the singing voice of Princess Jasmine from Aladdin and Fa Mulan for Mulan and Mulan II!

On Broadway, she has starred in:

  • Miss Saigon
  • Les Miserables
  • Sweeney Todd
  • Allegiance
  • The Flower Drum Song

Film and television credits include:

  • Yellow Rose
  • Pretty Little Liars
  • Crazy Ex-Girlfriend
  • The Voice Kids Philippines (judge)

Awards

  • Tony Award for Best Actress (Miss Saigon)
  • Disney Legend (Aladdin, Mulan & Mulan II)
  • Olivier Award (Miss Saigon)
  • Theatre World Awards (Miss Saigon)
